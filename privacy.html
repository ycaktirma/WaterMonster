Privacy Policy — WaterMonster

Effective date: 19.01.2026

WaterMonster respects your privacy. This app is designed to work without collecting or storing any personal user information.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

WaterMonster does not collect, store, or require:

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Account or login credentials
  • Any personally identifiable information

All hydration data and progress are stored locally on your device and never leave your phone.

2. Anonymous Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, such as:

  • App launches
  • Screen views
  • Onboarding completion
  • Feature interactions

This data does not identify you personally and is used only to improve app performance and user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. Anonymous analytics data is processed by Firebase in accordance with Google’s privacy policy.

4. Health Data

WaterMonster does not collect or process health data from Apple Health or other health services.

5. Data Security

Since all user data is stored locally on your device, you maintain full control over your hydration records. We take reasonable measures to protect anonymous analytics data.

6. Children’s Privacy

WaterMonster does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact: ycaktirma@gmail.com
